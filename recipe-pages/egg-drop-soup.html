Egg Drop Soup

Description

Egg drop soup is an asian soup made of egg. The way the soup is prepared gives a beautiful shape and texture to the egg. This recipe will use only spring onions as a topping but feel free to add whatever vegetables you wish.

Ingredients

  • Fish stock, msg, or any other broth stock
  • Soy sauce
  • Spring onion
  • Seasame oil

Steps

  1. In a pot mix 2 cups of water with your preferred broth stock. Stir in 1/2 tablespoon of soy sauce and 1/2 tablespoon of seasame oil. Heat it up until it boils.
  2. Whisk up an egg and chop up a spring onion
  3. When the broth starts boiling slowly pour in the beaten egg in a circular motion. You can use a laddle or spoon to make pouring easier.
  4. Pour soup into a bowl and handful of spring onion bits and enjoy!
