French Bread Pizza

Description

This recipe is for making a delicous french bread pizza. You will need an oven to bake the pizza bread. There are many ways to prepare a french bread pizza, this recipe is used for preparing a simple pepperoni pizza bread. Feel free to add any toppings not listed as you would a regular pizza. Below a list of ingredients, followed by directions on how it should be prepared.

Ingredients

  • French bread loaf
  • Tomato sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. First start by preheating your oven to 425 degrees F
  2. Slice your french bread loaf in half, like you are making a sandwich. Only one halve is needed, so you can save the other halve for next time.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly across the flat side of the bread.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce evenly.
  5. Place as much pepperoni as you wish on top of the cheese, making sure not stick them close together to ensure even cooking.
  6. When your oven is at baking temperature, put it inside and let it bake for 12 minutes.
  7. Once your pizza is done, let it cool down for a few minutes before eating. Enjoy!
