Spicy Peanut Noodles

Description

This recipe is for making spicy peanut noodles. This is an asian dish consisting of noodles enveloped in a rich spicy peanut sauce. Although this recipe is just for the noodles, feel free to add any protein and vegetables you wish.

Ingredients

  • Flat rice noodles
  • Asian chili oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Peanut butter

Steps

  1. Heat up 2 cups of water until it boils.
  2. Cook a handful of flat rice noodles in the boiling water for 5 minutes or until desired consistency
  3. To make the sauce add 1 tablespoon of soy sauce, 1 tablespoon of peanut butter, 1 tablespoon of chili oil and 2 tablespoons of the water used to cook the noodles all into a small bowl. Mix until it's a uniform color.
  4. When the noodles finish cooking, drain the water and place them into a pan.
  5. Pour the sauce into the pan of noodles and mix thoroughly until a noodles are evenly coated. If you have any protein or vegetables you want added, mix them in now.
  6. Top with your garnish of choice and enjoy!
